Beginners:
If you are new to strength training, you can often increase the weight by about 5-10% each week, depending on the lift and your comfort level.

Intermediate:
For those with some experience, a more moderate increase of about 2.5-5% per week is typical.

Advanced:
For experienced lifters, smaller increments of about 1-2% may be more appropriate, as gains become harder to achieve and require more careful management.
